Welcome to my blog. 


This is a simple space I’ve created to focus on writing without distractions. My goal here isn’t to gain a large audience but to practice writing.


I plan to primarily discuss different AI Governance and Ethics issues as well as post about interesting news. Here and there I will likely upload an essay or research paper I have written. (In fact my first three posts are previous essays.) It might also be sprinkled with off-topic posts.


This blog will likely evolve as I go, and I’m excited to see what takes shape. Thanks for taking a look!
